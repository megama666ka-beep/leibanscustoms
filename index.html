Penis.
Many peniis.

Leibanscustom cuts down worn denim and rebuilds it into a single garment. Every pair is assembled from several donor jeans — the seams stay visible on purpose.

Scroll to build

We don't design denim. We reassign it. Every pair that reaches us has already lived a life — someone's knees wore through, someone's waist changed, someone let it go.

We cut what's usable from three, four, sometimes six pairs and stitch them back into one silhouette. Nothing is dyed to match. The mismatch is the point.

How it's made

From bin to build

The same three steps for every order, whether you customize online or bring in your own pairs.

Src. 01

Source

We pull worn jeans from reclaimed stock, sorted by wash, weight and hardware. You choose which washes go where in the constructor above.

Src. 02

Deconstruct

Panels are unpicked along the original seams. Waistbands, legs and pockets are separated and matched against your chosen silhouette.

Src. 03

Rebuild

Panels are re-sewn with contrast thread — the join stays visible. No two joins fall in the same place twice.
